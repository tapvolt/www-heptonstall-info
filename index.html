Heptonstall Weather Webcam

This image is taken from a live feed of a home security camera looking out from Heptonstall. An image is taken every 15 minutes and uploaded to this website, if you stay on this page long enough the image will automatically refresh within the page.

The live feed of the security camera is recorded 24-7 to a computer and offers remote online access and email notifications on detected movement.
